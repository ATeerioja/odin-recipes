Ranch Chicken Tacos

Proper good looking tacos

These ranch chicken tacos are a great change from regular Mexican-style tacos. A quick, cool summer dinner made with leftover rotisserie chicken. Naturally, you can use any type of chicken cooked the way you like; just be sure to shred it for real tacos.

Ingredients

  • Ranch dressing
  • Sour cream
  • Taco shells
  • Green onions
  • Black olives
  1. Combine ranch dressing, sour cream
  2. Toss chicken with remaining taco seasoning.
  3. Warm tortillas in a skillet for about a minute on each side to make them pliable.
